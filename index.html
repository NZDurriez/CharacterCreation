Character Creation

Step 1: Open Your VMenu

Press F1 to open your VMenu. From here, navigate to Player Related Options → MP Ped Customization and choose between Create Male Character or Create Female Character. Make sure you save your character once you’re done. You can also choose to set as default character so that every time you respawn or load in, your saved appearance automatically loads.

Step 2: Character Appropriateness

Ensure your custom character is appropriate and sensible. Using any form of nudity is prohibited.

Step 3: Create Civilian Characters & Vehicles

Once you’ve completed the above steps, press T on your keyboard to open the chat. Type /civ and the Civilian Menu will open. Here you can create Civilian Characters and Civilian Vehicles information.

When clicking on Civilian Characters, you are required to enter a First Name and Last Name for your character (they must be appropriate and not offensive). Once entered, you’ll be prompted to enter your QP (Query Person).

Step 4: QP (Query Person) Templates

QP's are formatted specifically to provide police with information about your person. Below are two templates you can use and modify:

Example One:
"John Smith, Male Not Known Not Wanted, Nil Flags, Comes back to a 271 Address, Nil Actives, Class 1 Full Licence, 0 Demerits"

Example Two:
"John Smith, Male, Known and Wanted, Flags for carries weapons, Comes back to a 106 Address, Nil Actives, Class 1 Full Licence, 20 Demerits"

Step 5: Activate Your Character

Once you’ve entered your QP, your character is created. Next, you must mark your character as active. Although you can have multiple characters, only one can be active at any given time. Characters remain active only while you're in-game; therefore, when you rejoin the server, you'll need to use /civ again to log in to your desired character via the Civilian Characters menu.

Step 6: Create Vehicle Information (QVR)

QVR's (Query Vehicle Registration) are similar to QP's, except they provide police with information about your vehicle. Note: You must be in the vehicle that you want to register.

Example One:
"NHE318, 2010 Blue Holden Commodore, Current WOF - Expires 20/01/2024, Current Registration - Expires 05/04/2024. Registered owner a John Smith at a 271 Address"

Example Two:
"CEP155, 2005 White Toyota Celica, Current WOF - Expires 28/04/2024, Registration - Expired 19/11/2023. Registered owner a John Smith at a 106 Address. Certified for steering and suspension modification"

Step 7: Final Notes

Once you've completed the above steps correctly, you only need to provide your name to police whenever stopped; they can then search for your name and find the QP you entered. The same applies to any QVR the police search.

Information on how to save vehicle information is available in section 6 of the rules.
