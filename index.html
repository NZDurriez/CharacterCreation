Character Creation

Step 1: Open Your vMenu

  1. Press F1 to open your VMenu.
  2. Navigate to Player Related Options.
  3. Select MP Ped Customization and choose either Create Male Character or Create Female Character.
  4. Save your character when youâ€™re done. You may also choose to set it as the default so that every time you respawn or load in, your saved appearance automatically loads.

    5. Ensure your custom character is appropriate and sensible. Remember: Any form of nudity is prohibited.

Step 2: Create Civilian Characters & Vehicles

  1. Press T on your keyboard to open the chat.
  2. Type /civ to open the Civilian Menu.
  3. When selecting Civilian Characters, enter a First Name and Last Name (ensure these are appropriate and not offensive).
  4. You will be prompted to enter your QP (Query Person).
  5. Generate your QP using the QP/QVR Generator. You can access it here.
  6. Copy and paste the generated QP information into the /civ command.

Step 3: Activate Your Character

  1. After entering your QP, your character is created.
  2. Mark your character as active in the Civilian Characters menu.
  3. If you disconnect then rejoin the server later, You'll need to run the /civ command again to log in to your desired character.

Step 4: Create Vehicle Information (QVR)

  1. QVR (Query Vehicle Registration) works similarly to QP but is for your vehicle.
  2. Ensure you are inside the vehicle you wish to register.
  3. Generate your QVR using the QP/QVR Generator.
  4. Copy and paste the generated QVR information into the Civilian Vehicles section via /civ.

Step 5: Final Notes

    When stopped by the police, simply provide your name; they will search for your QP or QVR details. Information on how to save vehicle information is available in section 6 of the rules. If you need further assistance, please open a general support ticket on Discord.
